# Design System Specification: IWU AI Web Presence

## Overview
This design system provides a unified framework for the **AI Lab** (Academic Unit) and the **AI Club** (Student Org), ensuring both remain consistent with the holistic Indiana Wesleyan University brand while maintaining their unique institutional and student-facing identities.

## Colors
- **The AI Lab (Tier 2):** Uses **IWU Red (#A6192E)** as the anchor color for headers and institutional identifiers.
- **The AI Club (Tier 3):** Uses **IWU Spirit Red (#D11242)** to convey energy and student engagement.
- **Interactive Elements:** All buttons, links, and "Call to Action" elements must use **IWU Action (#09BDCB)**.
- **Contrast Standards:** Web content must maintain a contrast ratio of 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text to meet WCAG AAA standards.

## Typography
- **Headlines (High Impact):** **Tungsten** (All Caps) for bold, stacked headers that attract attention.
- **Headlines (Academic):** **Gotham Bold** for clear legibility and professional tone.
- **Body Copy:** **Gotham Book/Regular** for high legibility across all digital devices.
- **Accents/Quotes:** **Surveyor Text** for special callouts or mission-driven statements.

## Iconography
- **Library:** All web icons must be sourced from **FontAwesome**.
- **Exceptions:** Custom university-specific icons provided by **IWU Marketing** may be used if available.

## Digital Components
- **Buttons:** Solid **IWU Action (#09BDCB)** backgrounds with white text. Hover states should transition to **IWU Action Dark (#006685)**.
- **Cards & Containers:** Use **IWU Light Grey (#B2B4B2)** for subtle borders and **Action Light (#DEF1ED)** for highlighted feature sections.
- **Accessibility Requirements:** All images must have alt-text and videos must include captions to meet IWU's 100% Level A compliance target.

## Imagery & Tone
- **Voice:** Clear, concise, bold, and Christ-centered. Avoid clichés and institutional jargon.
- **Photography:** High-quality images that reflect authentic diversity and celebrate milestones.
